### 统计接口流量
```shell
awk '{print $1,$2}' access.log | sed -r 's/(.*?)\?(.*?)\s(.*)/\1 \3/g' | awk '{a[$1]+=$2}END{for (i in a)print i,a[i]*8/1024/1024"M"}' | sort -nrk 2 | head 
```
